Who We Are

GOMA Vocational Training Centre is a leading technical and vocational institution in Migori Town. Located at Kamadaga Centre – Oruba Kogenya – Nyangubo Road, we focus on equipping students with hands-on skills to meet industry standards.

Our Vision

To be a unique leading institution in providing quality professional and technical education.

Our Mission

To produce efficient, qualified and innovative technicians and professionals at affordable cost.

Our Core Values

Transparency, Professionalism, Integrity, Innovation and Accountability.
